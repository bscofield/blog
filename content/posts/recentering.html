I've been blogging on and off for ... heck, years now, and I've tried a lot of different approaches during that time. Among other things, I've had a fitness/running specific blog (which connected me to a wonderful, supportive community), I've had several work-specific blogs, I've tried microblogs of varying stripes - like I said, lots of different approaches. That said, I'm now trying another. For most of my professional life, my online presence has been centered around the domain culann.com. Given my current career direction, however, I'm resetting all of that - so going forward, culann will turn into more of a personal, microblogging site (and in fact, I'm probably going to move it over to tumblr), and my work life will revolve around this, benscofield.com. Hopefully it won't be too confusing to longtime (or new!) readers.
